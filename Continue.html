Each night you read over all of your notes about the strangeness around the space station.

Things have been going missing and you have noticed lots of things flickering like they are made of light rather than solid material.

One night while you are down in the basement talking to the human, you hear the door at the top of the stairs open.

You quickly hide in the tunnel but keep the door cracked so you can hear what's happening.

A soldier comes into view and you see him talking to the human. Nothing of interest happens so you are about to leave when you see the soldier trip on a box you left out.

For a brief second time stops and the soldier never hits the ground.

There is that weird flicker around him as he rights himself and then time resumes like nothing happened.

You are in shock but you manage to close the door quietly and make it to your room without being seen.
